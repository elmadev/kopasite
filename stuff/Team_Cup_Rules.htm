Team Cup 2004

Official Rules
First things first, sign up your team on the sign up page.

When the Cup starts, a gruop play will be played, in one or more gruops, depending on how many teams that paticipate.

A certain number of events will be played, and points are given after how good your team do.

Each player from the teams must choice a player number. This is just random. When a round starts the players can download a level. So that each player from the team have a different level.

Now you have a week to play the level, then send your result to TeamCup@pq.dk inlcuding replay, level file, your nick, team and time.

After a certain number of events, the 8 or 4 best team will qvalify for the ladder. They now have to compete for being the best team on the planet.
